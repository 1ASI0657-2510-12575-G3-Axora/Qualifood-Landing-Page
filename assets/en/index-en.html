Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the purpose of Qualifood?

Our app is a tool that allows you to manage each of the states of ingredients so they can be used for each dish in restaurants.

Can I use the app without an internet connection?

The app requires an internet connection to synchronize and share expenses. However, you can enter new expenses offline, and they will automatically synchronize when you connect to the internet.

Is there any cost to use the app?

The app offers a free plan with all basic features. We also have a premium plan with additional features like ad removal, advanced reports, and priority support.
